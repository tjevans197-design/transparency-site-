Our Mission

Transparency Affordable Housing Solutions is a nonprofit initiative committed to building modular, resident-controlled communities that are affordable, stable, and free from corporate exploitation. Our mission is to empower families and individuals with access to dignified homeownership through transparency, trust, and long-term stewardship.

Our Values

Our mission at Transparency Affordable Housing Solutions is to restore the American dream of homeownership by creating transparent, community-led housing alternatives that are affordable, secure, and free from private equity control. We believe every family deserves a place to call home — built with integrity, priced with honesty, and governed by the people who live there.

Vision Statement

We build communities — not portfolios. Transparency Affordable Housing Solutions exists to create safe, affordable, and resident-owned modular home neighborhoods that put people, not profits, first.
